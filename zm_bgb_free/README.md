# Free Gobblegums
This script allows you to despense Gobblegums for free.

# Features
* All Gobblegum machines will give you an amount of free Gobblegums per round.
* You can change the amount of free Gobblegums to despense using the dvar `bgb_free_amount`. The default amount is 3, no more than 10 and no less than 2. Execute the dvar and the amount and after so restart the match to take affect.
* Shows a HUD on the upper right corner of the screen of the amount of free Gobblegums available to depense.


# Note
The Gobblegum machine will display the incorrect price after the first hit each round.
